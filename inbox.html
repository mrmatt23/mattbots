⚙️ One-Time Setup

This page pulls submissions directly from Formspree using their API. Your credentials stay in your browser only.

  1. Sign up at formspree.io and create a new form.
  2. Copy your Form ID from the dashboard (e.g. xabcdefg).
  3. In recommend.html, replace YOUR_FORM_ID with your real Form ID.
  4. Go to Account → API Keys and generate a new key.
  5. Paste both below and click Load Inbox.
